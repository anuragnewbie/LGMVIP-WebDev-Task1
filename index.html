Nature is the precious asset of the lord. The lord has shared his asset amongst us and all the other living organisms. In earlier days humans used to use the leaves as their dress. The black rocks were used to ignite fire and that is how the fire was discovered. We exhale carbondioxide and we inhale oxygen that comes from the plants. If you light a candle and cover it with a glass over it, after few seconds the fire will vanish. This is because of the absence of oxygen. As everything is dependant on the nature whether its a living thing or a non - living thing then who are we to destroy the nature ? Lord is the epitome of this universe and if he has shared his most precious asset with us to make our lives simpler and easier to live then he has the full power to take it away from us as well. So respect the nature and preserve it.

A GLIMPSE OF MOTHER NATURE

    STEPS TO SAVE NATURE

    Plant more trees

    Try to plant more trees as this won't harm anyone instead it will gift new lives to all of us. This will also help in reducing the ecological imbalance. Plant new trees in your area and take good care of them.

    Stop cutting trees

    Trees are also a living being just like we humans and the other living organisms are, nobody other than the lord has the right to snatch their lives, so stop cutting down trees and stop others as well from commiting the same mistake.

    Be friendly to trees

    Today, we are surviving is just because of the presence of trees and in return we are taking up their lives. We should become a friend of trees and try to protect them instead. Trees provides us shade in a burining hot climate, fruits & vegetables to eat, oxygen to breathe, so being a human it is our responsibility to protect them and love them unconditionally.

    Save animals

    We humans have become the enemy of our nature and killing each other whether it be an animal or trees, we should stop this gruesome behaviour and save the animals from being tortured and killed and provide them shelter, food and love.

    Stop pollution

    We are the only ones who is destroying our mother nature by producing various harmful chemicals, gase that is making it difficult for us and all other living organisms to breathe and causing various skin diseases, we should stop producing harmful gases and start using eco-efficient gases and chemicals.

    Conserve water

    Water is life as the saying goes and we are wasting waters in a regular basis and if this trend continues, very soon we will have no water to drink causing in death of trees, animals, fish and other living organisms, We should start saving water by doing water harvesting to save and store rain water and many more other techniques.

    Our Nature 

    Nature is a blessing of Lord that they has bestowed to our lives. Respect it & preserve it. Love the nature.
